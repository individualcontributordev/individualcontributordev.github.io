CutScenes Removed Mod

Check out the CutScenes Removed (CSR) Mod for the PS1 version of Final Fantasy VII. This mod is designed to remove cutscenes from the game, providing a streamlined experience for players who prefer gameplay over cinematic sequences. It’s hosted on GitHub for easy access and contribution.
